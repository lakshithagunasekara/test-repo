Welcome to the IEEE Conference on Technology and Innovation. This conference is a premier event sponsored by the IEEE Industrial Electronics Society, aimed at bringing together the international community to present the latest research results, share new ideas and engineering breakthroughs, and discuss state-of-the-art challenges and future directions in technology and innovation.
